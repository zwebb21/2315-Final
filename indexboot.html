Introduction to my - portfolio Hello my name is Zarius. I self identify myself as a creator, a human entity whose purpose is to inspire and influence art, culture, and style into the Planet. I’m originally from Houston, TX and spend most of my time turning crazy geeky thoughts into real world manifestations. My journey as a entrepreneur and designer began with a quote that has stuck with me through multiple phases of my life, “ Do today what you want to see tomorrow.” It means take steps everyday of your life towards your goals you’ve set out for yourself and in my field of business you’re the boss.

All entrepreneurs know the amount of dedication and sacrifice is what gets you places, you have to make the hours on the clock compound which is why this quote help mold my mindset into what it is today. Everyday and every month of the year I spent learning and educating my self on skills and business tactics to increase my brands value. My vision is to build opportunities for myself and other businesses I want to see flourish or want to invest in using my creative skills. Creators are the future architects of society in my opinion, they will shape the way we live, view and interact with the world around us.
